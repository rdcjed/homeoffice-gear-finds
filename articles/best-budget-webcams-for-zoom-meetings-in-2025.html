1080p vs 720p: Is the Upgrade Worth It?

For most remote workers, 1080p Full HD is the sweet spot. The difference between a good 1080p webcam (like the Logitech C920) and a laptop's built-in 720p camera is night and day — sharper image, better colors, and much better low-light performance.

Don't Forget Lighting

Even the best webcam looks bad in poor lighting. Consider pairing your webcam with a simple ring light or desk lamp positioned in front of you (not from above or behind) for the best results.
