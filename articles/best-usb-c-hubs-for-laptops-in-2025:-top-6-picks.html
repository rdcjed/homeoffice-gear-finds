Best USB-C Hubs for Laptops in 2025: Top 6 Picks

#accessories #budgetgear #homeoffice

Most modern laptops — especially MacBooks and ultrabooks — come with only 2 or 3 USB-C ports. A USB-C hub is essential for connecting your monitor, peripherals, SD cards, and Ethernet all at once. But not all hubs are created equal.

We tested 18 USB-C hubs for data transfer speeds, HDMI output quality, power delivery stability, and build quality. Here are the 6 best USB-C hubs for home office use in 2025.

Key Features to Look For

Look for a hub with at least 4K 60Hz HDMI output (not just 30Hz), 100W power delivery passthrough so your laptop charges through the hub, and USB 3.0+ ports for fast file transfers. An Ethernet port is also highly recommended for stable video calls.
