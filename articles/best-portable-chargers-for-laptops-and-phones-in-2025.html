Best Portable Chargers for Laptops & Phones in 2025

#accessories #budgetgear #homeoffice

Running out of battery mid-meeting is every remote worker's nightmare. A quality portable charger ensures you can work from coffee shops, co-working spaces, or even outdoors without hunting for an outlet.

We tested power banks for actual capacity (manufacturer claims are often inflated), charging speed, portability, and build quality. Here are the best portable chargers for laptops and phones in 2025.

How Much Capacity Do You Need?

For charging phones only: 10000mAh is enough for 2-3 full charges. For laptop charging: look for 20000mAh+ with at least 45W USB-C Power Delivery output. A 25000mAh power bank can charge a MacBook Air completely from empty.

Don't Forget Air Travel Rules

If you travel, remember that power banks over 100Wh (roughly 27000mAh) are not allowed in carry-on luggage on most flights. All our picks above are under this limit.
